1.6 Playing MasterMind: "master-play.ss"

 (require htdp/master-play)

The teachpack implements the MasterMind game so that students can play the game
and get an understanding of what we expect from them.

(go name) → true
  name : symbol?
chooses a “secret” three-letter word, opens a canvas and a menu, and asks the
player to guess the word.
