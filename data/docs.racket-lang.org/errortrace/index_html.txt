Errortrace: Debugging and Profiling

Errortrace is a stack-trace-on-exceptions, profiler, and coverage tool for
Racket. It is not a complete debugger; DrRacket provides more. Meanwhile, using
Errortrace might be better than Racket’s limited stack-trace reporting.
