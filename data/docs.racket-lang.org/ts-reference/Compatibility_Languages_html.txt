9 Compatibility Languages

 #lang typed/scheme
 #lang typed/scheme/base
 #lang typed-scheme
Typed versions of the


    #lang scheme

and


    #lang scheme/base

languages. The


    #lang typed-scheme

language is equivalent to the


    #lang typed/scheme/base

language.
