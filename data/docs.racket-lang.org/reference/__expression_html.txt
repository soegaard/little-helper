2.4 Expression Wrapper: #%expression

(#%expression expr)
Produces the same result as expr. The only use of #%expression is to force the
parsing of a form as an expression.

Examples:

    > (#%expression (+ 1 2))
    3

    > (#%expression (define x 10))
    eval:496:0: define: not allowed in an expression context

    in: (define x 10)
