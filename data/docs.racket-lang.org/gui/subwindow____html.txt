subwindow<%> : interface?
  implements:  subarea<%>
               window<%>
A subwindow<%> is a containee window.

(send a-subwindow reparent new-parent) → void?
   new-parent   :   (or/c (is-a?/c frame%) (is-a?/c dialog%)
                          (is-a?/c panel%) (is-a?/c pane%))
Removes the window from its current parent and makes it a child of new-parent.
The current and new parents must have the same eventspace, and new-parent
cannot be a descendant of a-subwindow.

If a-subwindow is deleted within its current parent, it remains deleted in
new-parent. Similarly, if a-subwindow is shown in its current parent, it is
shown in new-parent.
